League Advisor

League Advisor is a lightweight software that requires no installation. When computing your advice it will consume CPU resources for a very short time on a single core as to not exhaust your computer. These computations would be too heavy to perform on our servers for everyone, hence the need for a software. If the product gets popular we might consider porting it to javascript.
